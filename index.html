Ta Anh Nguyen

My name is Ta Anh Nguyen, born in 2006 in Gia Lai, and I am currently living and working in Ho Chi Minh City. With a deep passion for both technology and art, I am a versatile and creative individual specializing in various fields such as programming (coder), graphic design (designer), photography (photographer), video editing (editor), and cinematography (camera operator). Known for my ability to blend technical expertise with artistic creativity, I not only develop websites but also create visually stunning graphics and captivating videos.

With determination and a growth mindset, I am always looking for ways to explore and improve my skills, continuously learning to deliver the highest quality work. I strive to combine creativity in my design and video production while utilizing my programming skills to create innovative and effective solutions. Thanks to my passion and diverse skill set, I am steadily establishing myself through both personal projects and professional work.
